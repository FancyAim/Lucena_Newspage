Diocese of Lucena Christmas Party 2024

Festive Decorations

In preparation for their Christmas party, the class officers created beautiful hand-made decorations and balloons to adorn the classroom. They also crafted a delightful photo booth with cute headbands and printed hand-held signs, making every corner of the room radiate festive warmth.

Exciting Activities

The celebration included engaging games such as "In, Out, Left, Right," where participants moved in sync, causing bursts of laughter. Another highlight was guessing classmates' baby pictures, bringing nostalgia and fun to the event.

A unique game, "Eh, ikaw?", tested creativity and quick thinking, with duos mimicking actions and describing them in Tagalog. Winners received loot bags filled with candies and snacks, generously prepared by students.

Gift-Giving and Performances

During the party, students exchanged gifts, with heartfelt performances adding to the festive mood. Even those without gifts felt the warmth of the Christmas spirit as classmates sang beautifully and celebrated together.

Shared Meals and Prayers

The celebration concluded with a heartfelt prayer, followed by a communal meal. Students ate, laughed, and strengthened their bonds, leaving no room for solitude and reinforcing a sense of community.
